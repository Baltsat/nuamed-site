How it works

A loop, not a program.

01

Learn each person's normal

We read what your environment already collects — wearables, rooms, check-ins, the shape of a week. No new hardware to start, no forms, no onboarding theater.

02

Move at the right moment

When a weak signal drifts — sleep shortening, recovery flattening — the system acts then, quietly, through whatever your place already offers. Not a broadcast schedule. A moment, chosen.

03

Say what happened

Every intervention is logged and explained back in plain language — kept if it worked, stopped if it didn't, with the reason either way. The system gets better; the person sees why.

Weekly readbackSun 19:00
NUAMED Your deep sleep has been under your usual range for four nights — it lines up with the later dinners this week. I moved your sauna slot to 17:30 and kept the earlier kitchen reminder. Wednesday and Thursday came back to normal.
NUAMED The cold-plunge suggestion from last month isn't earning its place — no measurable effect on your recovery in three weeks. I've stopped it.
Nothing needed from you. Reply if anything felt off.
